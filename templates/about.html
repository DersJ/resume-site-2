About Anders

Senior Cloud Services Engineer at Teradata

I graduated from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill in May of 2020 with a BS in Computer Science. As a problem solver since birth, I love using my programming and engineering skills to engineer solutions to real world problems.

I've been working at Teradata since 2019. After starting as a Software Engineering Intern, I came back to work full time post graduation. Currently, I'm a Senior Cloud Engineer working on the Cloud User Experience team. I build the UIs (Angular) and APIs (GraphQL) that comprise Vantage Console, an analytics & operations platform for Teradata cloud systems. Vantage Console integrates with all 3 major cloud providers to provide administors a single pane of glass interface to manage their deployments. As our team name implies, we are responsible for creating and maintaining an excellent user experience for a wide variety of personas.

Additionally, I participate in the development of our open source UI platfrom, Covalent.

Outside of , I'm an professional Ultimate Player for the Carolina Flyers. We won the 2021 AUDL Championship, taking down the New York Empire in the final. I finished the 2021 season as the leauge's 2nd highest goal scorer and was named AUDL Rookie of the Year.
