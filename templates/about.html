About Anders

Senior Full Stack Engineer at Unchained

I graduated from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill in May of 2020 with a BS in Computer Science. As a problem solver since birth, I love using my programming and engineering skills to engineer solutions to real world problems.

I view Bitcoin as the most significant invention in my lifetime. It offers an alternative to the manipulated and corrupt fiat financial system. To unlock Bitcoin's full potential, a parallel infrastructure of financial services is essential. At Unchained, we are at the forefront of creating these tools. Our products include Bitcoin collaborative custody, loan offerings, and retirement accounts.

Prior to joining Unchained, I worked at Teradata. I began as a Software Engineering Intern and advanced to the role of Senior Cloud Engineer within the Cloud User Experience team. There, my team developed the Vantage Console, an analytics and operations platform designed for Teradata's cloud systems. The Vantage Console is compatible with the three major cloud providers, offering administrators a unified interface to manage their deployments. In addition, I contributed to the creation of our open-source UI platform, Covalent.

I'm also a professional ultimate player for the Carolina Flyers. We won the 2021 AUDL Championship, taking down the New York Empire in the final. I finished the 2021 season as the leauge's 2nd highest goal scorer and was named AUDL Rookie of the Year.
